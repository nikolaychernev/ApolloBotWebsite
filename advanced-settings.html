Advanced Settings

The advanced settings are accessible from the toggle in the top-right corner of the settings screen. They are only intended to be changed if the extension stops working in case Instagram™ change their APIs. Until the extension gets updated you are welcome to adjust the settings yourself in order to fix the issue.

Step 1

Open your Instagram™ profile and press F12 on your keyboard. On the new window which appeared make sure the "Network" tab and "XHR" are selected. Write "query" in the search bar.

Step 2

Click "followers" on your profile while keeping the window from step 1 open.

Step 3

Click the new row which appeared in the window from step 1.

Step 4

Make sure the "Headers" tab is selected.

Step 5

Scroll to the bottom where you will see "x-ig-app-id" and "query_hash" parameters. Copy their values to the "Application Id" and "Load Followers Query Hash" settings respectively. Repeat steps 2 to 5 for the other query hash settings if the specific actions don't work.
